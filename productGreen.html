GeneType for Breast Cancer

GeneType tests evaluate the risk of developing Breast Cancer, even without a family history. ($349 AUD)

  • Home collection test kit.
  • Results in 3-4 weeks.
  • Easy cheek swab.
  • Secure, accurate results delivered by a health professional.

85% of women diagnosed with Breast Cancer have little to no family history

Many factors continue to Breast Cancer risk. Our patented and proprietary algorithm combines your DNA, family history, and clinical data (mammography-based breast density).

DNA
Small changes in our DNA that can increase our risk for cancer
Age
Cancer risk increases with age
Family History
Medical background about your risk for cancer
Additional Factors
Menopausal status and breast density, can impact your risk
